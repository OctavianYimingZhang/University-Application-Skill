# Application Visa

Read `references/evidence-contract.md` and `references/submission.md`.

## Workflow

1. Identify the destination jurisdiction, applicant citizenship or residence, study level, programme dates, and current application or offer stage.
2. Use current official immigration or government sources for visa rules and university sources for institution-issued sponsorship documents and institution-specific steps.
3. Separate official requirements, university processes, applicant-held evidence, deadlines, costs, and unresolved facts.
4. Record the source URL, jurisdiction, applicable date or cycle, and access date for each mutable rule.
5. Return the requested explanation, evidence table, blocker summary, timeline, or action plan.

Present routine administrative information and source-backed next actions. Direct unusual immigration history, legal ambiguity, refusals, or status disputes to the official authority or a qualified professional.

Use the user's requested output language; otherwise use English.
